The Bahamas

The Bahamas is a stunning chain of islands in the Atlantic Ocean, just off the coast of Florida. Known for its crystal-clear turquoise waters, white-sand beaches, and laid-back island vibe, it's a place where time seems to slow down. Made up of over 700 islands and cays, though only about 30 are inhabited, it offers everything; from bustling towns like Nassau, to quiet untouched stretches of paradise. Rich in culture and history, The Bahamas is a melting pot of African, European, and Caribbean influences that is reflected in its music, food, and friendly people. Whether you're snorkeling over vibrant coral reefs, enjoying fresh conch salad by the sea, or just relaxing in the sun, every island feels like a true escape.
