RAPIDS WHITE WATER RAFTING

Here at Rapids White Water Rafting, we are passionate about providing thrilling and unforgettable rafting experiences. Our team of experienced guides is dedicated to ensuring your safety and enjoyment on the water.

Whitewater rafting's history spans centuries, evolving from necessity-driven river travel to a popular recreational activity. Early uses involved Native American tribes using rafts for fishing and transportation on rivers like the Columbia and Snake. Later, explorers like Lt. John Fremont used rubber rafts for expeditions in the 1800s.

HISTORY

The sport's commercialization began in the mid-20th century with the Rockefellers using surplus military rafts for guest excursions on the Snake River. Modern commercial rafting companies emerged in the 1960s and 70s, and whitewater rafting was featured in the 1972 Munich Olympics.
